## Case Study Examples

### Heritage Church Restoration

**Challenge:** Full façade restoration of 1890s church with archaeological sensitivity
**Solution:** Independent structure spanning heritage grave sites with cantilever to reach upper elements
**Result:** Zero impact on archaeological features, full access achieved

### Curved Commercial Building

**Challenge:** Access to curved glass facade for cleaning system installation
**Solution:** Segmented platform system following building curve with specialized edge protection
**Result:** Continuous access along entire curved elevation

### Bridge Inspection Access

**Challenge:** Inspection access for bridge with river below
**Solution:** Suspended platform system from bridge deck with engineered anchor points
**Result:** Full inspection access without waterway impact
