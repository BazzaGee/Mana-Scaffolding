# The True Cost of Scaffolding Accidents

Safety failures on scaffolding have costs far beyond the immediate incident. Understanding these costs drives better safety decisions.

## Human Costs

### To the Worker

**Immediate:**
- Injury severity varies from minor to fatal
- Pain and suffering
- Medical treatment
- Recovery time

**Long-term:**
- Permanent disability possibilities
- Chronic pain conditions
- Psychological impact
- Career implications
- Quality of life changes

### To the Family

**Immediate:**
- Emotional trauma
- Hospital visits and support
- Family disruption

**Long-term:**
- Caregiver responsibilities
- Income impacts
- Lifestyle adjustments
- Ongoing support needs

### To the Workplace

**Team Impact:**
- Witnessing trauma affects others
- Morale consequences
- Safety culture damage
- Confidence in employer shaken

## Legal Costs

### Regulatory Response

**WorkSafe Investigation:**
- Site inspection and potential shutdown
- Document review and interviews
- Enforcement actions possible
- Legal proceedings if serious

**Potential Outcomes:**
- Improvement notices
- Prohibition notices (site shutdown)
- Infringement notices (fines)
- Prosecution for serious breaches

### Prosecution Consequences

**Penalties:**
- Fines up to $500,000 for companies
- Up to $100,000 for individuals
- Reparations to victims
- Court costs

**Additional Effects:**
- Criminal record for individuals
- Reputation damage
- Future contract implications
- Insurance impacts

### Civil Liability

**Personal Grievance:**
- Employee claims against employer
- Compensation through ACC or beyond
- Legal representation costs

**Third Party Claims:**
- Public liability exposures
- Property damage claims
- Professional indemnity claims
- Contractual breach claims

## Financial Costs

### Direct Costs

**Medical:**
- Treatment expenses (ACC covered)
- Rehabilitation costs
- Ongoing care requirements
- Specialized treatment

**Project:**
- Site shutdown during investigation
- Delay costs
- Replacement labor
- Contractual penalties
- Insurance premium increases

**Administrative:**
- Investigation compliance
- Legal representation
- Insurance claims management
- Documentation and reporting

### Indirect Costs

**Productivity:**
- Lost time from injured worker
- Impact on other workers
- Management time diverted
- Training replacement staff

**Reputation:**
- Client confidence affected
- Future tender impacts
- Industry standing
- Community perception

**Culture:**
- Safety morale impact
- Retention difficulties
- Recruitment challenges
- Team dynamics changes
